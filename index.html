FAQ

Everything You're Wondering.

How does LinkedIn ghostwriting work?
We start with a discovery call where I learn about you, your goals, and your industry. From there, I handle everything — research, writing, and revisions. You review the posts, give feedback, and I refine until they're perfect. Then you simply publish.
Will the content actually sound like me?
Absolutely. Before I write a single word, I invest time understanding how you speak, what you value, and what makes your perspective unique. I study your existing content, past interviews, or conversations. The goal is zero gap between your real voice and what appears on LinkedIn.
How many revisions are included?
As many as it takes. I don't put a hard limit on revisions — I want you to be genuinely proud of every post that goes out under your name. In practice, most posts need 1-2 rounds of light tweaks before you love them.
Who owns the content?
You do — 100%. Once a post is delivered and approved, it belongs entirely to you. I never republish, repurpose, or reference client content publicly without explicit permission.
How does the free 1-week trial work?
Simple: reach out, we jump on a quick intro call, and I write posts for you for one full week at no cost. You see exactly what the experience is like — the quality, the turnaround, the collaboration. No credit card, no contract, no pressure. You decide if you want to continue.
How do we communicate and collaborate?
Most clients prefer WhatsApp or email for daily communication, with a shared Google Doc or Notion board for content review. I adapt to whatever workflow fits your life. I'm responsive, async-friendly, and always meet deadlines.
