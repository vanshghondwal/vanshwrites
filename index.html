FAQ

Everything You're Wondering.

How does LinkedIn ghostwriting work?
We start with a discovery conversation where I learn about you, your goals, and your industry. From there, I handle research, writing, and revisions. You review the posts, share feedback, and I refine until they're right. Then you simply publish.
Will the content actually sound like me?
That's the entire job. Before writing a single word, I invest time understanding how you speak, what you believe, and what makes your perspective unique. I study any existing content or conversations you share. The goal is no gap between your real voice and what appears on LinkedIn.
How many revisions are included?
As many as it takes. I don't put a hard limit on revisions — I want you to be genuinely proud of every post. In practice, most posts need 1–2 rounds of light tweaks before they feel right.
Who owns the content?
You do — 100%. Once a post is delivered and approved, it belongs entirely to you. I never republish, repurpose, or reference client content publicly without explicit permission.
How does the free 1-week trial work?
Reach out, we jump on a quick intro call, and I write posts for you for one full week at no cost. No credit card, no contract, no pressure. You experience the full process — the quality, the turnaround, the collaboration — and decide from there.
How do we communicate during the process?
Most clients prefer WhatsApp or email for daily communication, with a shared Google Doc or Notion board for content review. I adapt to whatever workflow fits your life — responsive, async-friendly, and always on time.
